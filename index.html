FAQ

What is a SnapInk™ strap sleeve?

A semi-rigid, print-ready cover that slides over a standard snapback strap, hides the pegs, and turns the strap into a clean branding surface. The system is patent-pending.

Will it fit my hat?

V1 sleeves target common snap spacing (~7 mm CTC). We’ll publish a fit guide and multiple sizes. Final fit will be validated during prelaunch wear-tests.

Can I print my logo on it?

Yes. The exterior is engineered for UV/pad printing, emboss, or foil. Creator customization opens after Drop 001.

When does Drop 001 ship?

We’ll announce the date to the waitlist first. We’re completing wear-tests and finalizing the supply chain. Join the waitlist for early access.

SnapInk™ Fit Guide

Our sleeves fit standard snapback hats. If your hat uses a Yupoong / Flexfit strap (industry standard), SnapInk™ will fit. That includes New Era, Richardson, Otto, Decky, and most streetwear blanks. They do not fit fitteds, strapbacks, or Velcro hats. Final compatibility will be confirmed during prelaunch testing.

✅ Compatible

  • Yupoong / Flexfit
  • New Era 9FIFTY
  • Richardson
  • Otto Cap
  • Decky
  • Adidas/Nike/Champion snapbacks

⚠️ Might Not Fit

  • Cheap fast-fashion hats
  • Vintage 80s/90s snapbacks
  • Youth sizes

❌ Not Compatible

  • Fitted hats
  • Strapbacks (buckle)
  • Velcro backs
  • Elastic band truckers
