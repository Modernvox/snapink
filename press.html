Boston, MA — Patent Pending — September 9, 2025

SnapInk™ Unveils the World’s First Interchangeable Snapback Strap Sleeve

For more than 70 years, the plastic back strap of a snapback hat has been ignored. SnapInk™ is changing that with a patent-pending, precision-molded TPU sleeve that slides over the existing strap, hides the pegs, and delivers a premium, seamless look. The back strap becomes prime real estate for color, graphics, and identity.

Industry watchers are calling SnapInk “the biggest branding opportunity in hats since the 1950s.” With production costs under $3 at scale and a $24.99 target retail, SnapInk positions itself as a high-margin accessory with strong potential across licensing, collaborations, and limited drops.

SnapInk’s first release, Drop 001, is in prelaunch with a waitlist now open. Waitlisters will be notified 24 hours before the drop goes live.

Who it’s for

  • Streetwear collectors and sneakerheads seeking a new canvas
  • Sports teams, artists, and brands looking for visible, eye-level branding
  • Creators who want swappable, collectible merch

Key features

  • Interchangeable, precision-molded TPU sleeve — semi-rigid yet flexible
  • Hides snap pegs, preserves normal fit and adjustability
  • Engineered exterior for print/emboss/foil applications (post-launch)
